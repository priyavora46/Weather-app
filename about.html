About This Weather App

This Weather App was built to provide quick and accurate weather forecasts for your city. It includes temperature, wind, and condition information for today, tomorrow, and the next 5 days.

The app is developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap with real-time data fetched from an open weather API.

Whether you're planning your trip or just curious about the weather, this app will give you a reliable update anytime, anywhere!
